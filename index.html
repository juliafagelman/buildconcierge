Our 10-Day Action Plan

Phase 1: Discovery & Product Training (3 Days)

A deep dive into the product, users, and ecosystem to establish a solid foundation.

  • Define Critical Information: We will collaboratively identify the absolute essential information that early adopters need for a successful start. This involves mapping out the "first-hour" experience to ensure it's smooth and productive.
  • Understand User Profiles: Conduct sessions to gain a deep understanding of the early adopting customers. This will help tailor the tone, complexity, and focus of the educational materials to their specific needs and technical abilities.
  • Select & Prepare Tools: Evaluate and decide on the optimal tools for content creation (e.g., help authoring tools, screen recording software) and hosting. We will also organize any necessary licenses to ensure a smooth workflow.
  • Product Immersion: Engage in intensive product training sessions. The goal is to thoroughly understand the workflow builder's capabilities, current limitations, and the specific terminology to be used consistently across all documentation.

Phase 2: Content Planning (1 Day)

Strategically outlining the initial content to maximize impact and clarity.

  • Article Structure Definition: We will define a comprehensive list of articles to be produced. The initial plan is to create a dedicated, detailed article for each individual block within the workflow builder to ensure granular, easy-to-find information.
  • Identify Core Use Cases: Brainstorm and select the most popular and straightforward customer use cases. These examples will be woven throughout the articles to provide practical context and demonstrate value.
  • Content Prioritization: Based on the identified use cases, we will decide on the priority and timeline for article creation, ensuring that the most critical content is developed first to unblock early users.

Phase 3: Content Creation (3 Days)

Executing the content plan and producing high-quality initial documentation.

  • Focused Article Production: Dedicated time for writing the help articles in the order of priority determined in Phase 2. This will involve clear, concise writing, screenshots, and embedding the use case examples.
  • Video Walkthrough Scripting: Develop a detailed, engaging script for a video walkthrough of the workflow builder's core functionality. The script will serve as the foundation for a future video production.

Phase 4: Feedback & Iteration (2 Days)

Refining the initial drafts through collaborative review to ensure accuracy and clarity.

  • Deliver Drafts for Review: The initial set of created articles and the first draft of the video script will be delivered to the Build Concierge team for review.
  • Iterative Feedback Loop: We will collect detailed feedback on the content's accuracy, clarity, and tone. This phase is crucial for making necessary adjustments and ensuring the content meets the required standard.
  • Final Delivery: Based on the feedback, the content will be revised and the final versions of all articles and the script will be delivered, ready for use.

Phase 5: Scaling & Next Steps (1 Day)

Establishing a long-term strategy for customer education and content development.

  • Future Resource Roadmap: Develop a strategic roadmap for the creation of further resources, aligned with the upcoming product release plan to ensure documentation keeps pace with new features.
  • Customer Education Strategy: Build a high-level roadmap for the overall customer education offering at Build Concierge, considering different formats and channels for learning.
  • Establish Feedback Mechanism: Once initial resources are shared with customers, we will define a clear process for collecting their feedback to continuously improve the documentation and understand what is missing.

Success Metrics

  • Positive feedback from customers on helpfulness and usability of resources.
  • Reduction in support emails.
