Our 10-Day Action Plan

Phase 1: Discovery & product training (3 days)

  • Define Critical Information: Define what is the most important information for early adopters to successfully get started with Build Concierge's workflow builder.
  • Understand User Profiles: Understand who are the early adopting customers who will be using the resources to be created.
  • Select & Prepare Tools: Determine the optimal tools for content creation (e.g., help authoring tools, screen recording software) and hosting, and organize any necessary licences.
  • Product Immersion: Engage in intensive product training sessions. The goal is to thoroughly understand the workflow builder's capabilities, current limitations, and the specific terminology to be used consistently across all documentation.

Phase 2: Content planning (1 day)

  • Article Structure Definition: Define a comprehensive list of articles to be produced. The initial plan is to create a dedicated, detailed article for each block within the workflow builder to ensure granular, easy-to-find information.
  • Identify Core Use Cases: Brainstorm and select the most popular and straightforward customer use cases. These examples will be woven throughout the articles to provide practical context and demonstrate value.
  • Content Prioritization: Based on the use cases, decide on the priority and timeline for article creation, ensuring that the most critical content is developed first to unblock early users.

Phase 3: Content creation (3 days)

  • Focused Article Production: Dedicated time for writing the help articles in the order of priority determined in Phase 2. This will involve clear, concise writing, screenshots, and embedding use case examples.
  • Video Walkthrough Scripting: Develop a detailed, engaging script for a video walkthrough of the workflow builder's core functionality. The script will serve as the foundation for a future video production.

Phase 4: Feedback and iteration (2 days)

  • Deliver Drafts for Review: The initial set of articles and the first draft of the video script delivered for review.
  • Iterative Feedback Loop: Collect feedback on the content's accuracy, clarity, and tone.
  • Final Delivery: Based on the feedback, the content will be revised and the final versions of all articles and the script will be delivered, ready for use.

Phase 5: Scaling (1 day)

  • Future Resource Roadmap: Develop a strategic roadmap for the creation of further resources, aligned with the upcoming product release plan to ensure documentation keeps pace with new features.
  • Establish Feedback Mechanism: Once initial resources are shared with customers, we will define a clear process for collecting their feedback to continuously improve the documentation and understand what is missing.

Next steps: Following trial

If we will continue to work together following the trial, the next steps will be to:

  • Video Walkthrough: Record video walkthrough according to finalized script.
  • Future Resource Roadmap: Execute the roadmap prepared in Phase 5 to create additional articles and resources according to product roadmap.
  • Customer Education Strategy: Build a high-level long-term roadmap for the overall customer education offering at Build Concierge, considering different formats and channels for learning.
